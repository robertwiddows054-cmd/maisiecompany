Infrastructure Services Through Structured Coordination

MaisieCompany designs and operates coordination systems for infrastructure-critical services, including HVAC, life safety, and other regulated building systems.

MaisieSystem

MaisieSystem is the operating platform developed by MaisieCompany. It coordinates infrastructure services such as HVAC, fire & life safety, and compliance-driven building systems through structured processes and intelligent workflows.

MaisieSystem is designed to support increasing automation and system intelligence over time.

Services

Current managed services delivered through MaisieSystem include:

Fire & Life Safety Management

Scheduling, coordination, and documentation of fire and life-safety obligations. MaisieSystem ensures inspections are tracked, contractors are coordinated, and compliance records are always audit-ready.

This service represents the first implementation of MaisieSystem's coordination model.

MaisieSystem does not carry out regulated inspections or certifications.
